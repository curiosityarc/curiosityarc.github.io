Our Story

Curiosity Arc Foundation was born from a simple, powerful belief — that curiosity sparks progress. Founded with the mission to catalyze technology research in India, we are driven by a vision of using science and innovation to solve real-world challenges, especially in the sustainability and India-impact space.

In a world full of complex problems, we exist to ask bold questions, fund meaningful exploration, and turn ideas into impactful solutions.

This is just the beginning of our journey — and we're building an arc of curiosity that leads to change.

Vision

An India that leads the world through bold, breakthrough research—solving its own challenges and inspiring sustainable progress globally.

Our Mission

To catalyze technology research and development projects that drive lasting impact in India and promote sustainability.
