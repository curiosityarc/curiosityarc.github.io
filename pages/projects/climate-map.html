Project Overview

The World Climate Map project is an innovative open-source platform that combines artificial intelligence with climate data to create interactive educational experiences. Our goal is to make complex climate science accessible and engaging for everyone, from students to policymakers.

Platform Features

Interactive 3D Globe

Explore climate data through an immersive 3D visualization experience

Real-time Data

Access up-to-date climate information from various global sources

Historical Analysis

Study climate patterns and trends throughout history

AI Predictions

View AI-powered future climate scenario modeling

Access comprehensive lesson plans and teaching materials
