World Climate Map

Open source AI portal for interactive climate education and visualization

Project Overview

The World Climate Map project is an innovative open-source platform that combines artificial intelligence with climate data to create interactive educational experiences. Our goal is to make complex climate science accessible and engaging for everyone, from students to policymakers.

Platform Features

  • Interactive 3D globe visualization
  • Real-time climate data integration
  • Historical climate pattern analysis
  • Future climate scenario modeling
  • Educational resources and lesson plans
